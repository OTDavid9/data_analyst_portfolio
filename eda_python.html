INTRODUCTION TO THE PROJECT¶

Ta-Feng Store is a Chinese grocery store that keep daily record of products sold with their respective sales prices. The format of the store data collection is as follows: TRANSACTION_DT, CUSTOMER_ID, AGE_GROUP, PIN_CODE, PRODUCT_SUBCLASS, PRODUCT_ID, AMOUNT, ASSET, SALES_PRICE.

My interest is to explore the transactions of Ta-Feng Store, with the aim of answering question which will help see the pattern in the transaction of Ta-Feng Store.

Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)¶

Data analysis is one of the engine powers of a successful business, it is important to note that in order to make data-driven decision for businesses, the relevance of data analytics can not be overemphasized.

EDA is a process of analyzing data sets to summarize their main characteristics, often using statistical methods to discover pattern in the data set and other data visualization methods. EDA gives an overview of what our data is capable of doing and to guide the process of answering research questions.

The project will see me demonstrating the tools of EDA learned during the course of my learning at: https://jovian.ai/learn/data-analysis-with-python-zero-to-pandas through the use of relevant tools such: Python Libraries(Numpy, Pandas, Seaborn, Matplotlib and so on), Jupyter Notebook

Details about the dataset¶

The dataset contains a Chinese grocery store transaction data from November 2000 to February 2001.

Data source: https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/chiranjivdas09/ta-feng-grocery-dataset

Features of data set¶

  • TRANSACTION_DATE
  • CUSTOMER_ID
  • AGE_GROUP
  • PIN_CODE
  • PRODUCT_SUBCLASS
  • PRODUCT_ID
  • AMOUNT
  • ASSET
  • SALES_PRICE

Inferences and Conclusion¶

Write some explanation here: a summary of all the inferences drawn from the analysis, and any conclusions you may have drawn by answering various questions.

Inferences¶

  • Customers whose age are less than 25 have the highest average profit in the store.
  • There is significant difference between the profits made on various transaction days.
  • Some products are more highly profitable while some product only gives rise to enormous loss.
  • Some customers have great asset that contribute to the positive profit growth
  • There is a positve relationship between ASSET and PROFIT which is about .83825

Conclusion¶

  • Since large percentage of profit comes from customer whose age are less than 25, it is important to give priority to this fraction of the customer.
  • Each days of the week with lesser profit should be studied to understand what factors contributed to it.
  • Products with high profit values should be made available in stock always.
  • Customers with great asset should be wooed the more to buy profitable products.
  • The relations of customers with great asset implies that the higher the asset the higher the profit.
